Programmed by Joseph Horsmann and Veronica Lesnar

W moves the camera forward
S moves the camera backwards
A moves the camera to the left
D moves the camera to the right
Q moves the camera up
E moves the camera down
Z switches to perspective view
X switches to orthographic view
1 changes pitch
2 changes pitch in opposite direction
3 changes roll
4 changes roll in opposite direction
5 changes yaw
6 changes yaw in opposite direction
